Lists Example

Shopping List:
- Apples
- Bananas
- Oranges
- Grapes

Nested List:
- Category A
  - Item A1
  - Item A2
- Category B
  - Item B1
  - Item B2

Numbered List:
1. First step
2. Second step
3. Third step

Mixed List:
1. Primary item
   - Sub-item 1
   - Sub-item 2
2. Another primary
   - Another sub-item

Alphabetic List:
a. Option A
b. Option B
c. Option C

Alternative Numbered Format:
1) First item
2) Second item
3) Third item

Unicode Bullets:
• First item
• Second item
• Third item
